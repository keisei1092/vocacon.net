WHAT IS VOCACON?

The World Vocaloid Convention (VOCACON) is one of largest Vocaloid fan-made conventions for all vocaloid fan and is held at Yamaha Resort Tsumagoi in Japan.
The latest vocaloid-related culture from all over the world will gather here on Feb 21-22, 2015.
EVENT INFORMATION

Event Title: VOCACON 2015 - The first World Vocaloid Convention
Date: February 21(Sat) - 22(Sun), 2015
Location: Yamaha Resort Tsumagoi (in Kakegawa City, Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan)
Organizer: Vocacon Executive Committee
Inquiries: vocacon@gmail.com

Global Hashtag: #vocacon
Official Twitter: @vocacon
EVENT CONTENT

Among the 2 days, about 40 events will be shown by various exhibitors for vocaloid fan, such as live performance, concert, DJ event, interactive session, talk show, workshop, etc.
